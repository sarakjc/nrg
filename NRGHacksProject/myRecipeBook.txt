Title:
Avocado Toast
Ingredients:
1 ripe avocado
2 slices of bread (whole wheat, sourdough, or your favorite type)
Salt, to taste
Pepper, to taste
Optional: Red pepper flakes, lemon juice, olive oil, or your favorite toppings (e.g., a fried egg, cherry tomatoes, or feta cheese)
Steps:
1. Toast the bread for 2 min in the oven.

2. Peel and mash the avocado with a fork.

3. Season the avocado with the salt, pepper, and other spices.

4. Spread the avocado on the toast.

5. Top with some garnish, and serve!
