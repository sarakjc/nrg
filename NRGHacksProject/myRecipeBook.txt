Title:
Avocado Toast
Ingredients:
1 ripe avocado
2 slices of bread (whole wheat, sourdough, or your favorite type)
Salt, to taste
Pepper, to taste
Optional: Red pepper flakes, lemon juice, olive oil, or your favorite toppings (e.g., a fried egg, cherry tomatoes, or feta cheese)
Steps:
Toast the bread
Mash the avocado with a fork
Season the avocado with the salt, pepper, and other spices
Spread the avocado on the toast

Title:
Brownies
Ingredients:
1½ cups granulated sugar
¾ cup all-purpose flour
⅔ cup cocoa powder
½ cup powdered sugar
½ cup dark chocolate chips
¾ teaspoon sea salt
2 large eggs
½ cup extra-virgin olive oil
2 tablespoons water
½ teaspoon vanilla extract
Steps:
Preheat the oven to 325°F. Lightly spray an 8x8 baking dish with cooking spray and line it with parchment paper. Spray the parchment paper.
In a medium bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, chocolate chips, and salt.
In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, olive oil, water, and vanilla.
Sprinkle the dry mix over the wet mix and stir until just combined.
Pour the batter into the prepared pan and use a spatula to smooth the top. Bake for 40 to 45 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out with only a few crumbs attached

Title:
Fried Noodles
Ingredients:
1 package of rice noodles
2 carrots 
1 handful bean sprouts
2 onions
5 napa cabbage leaves
4 eggs
2 tbs soy sauce
1 tbs oyster sauce
1 tsp sugar
Steps:
Boil noodles
Mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sugar
Julienne carrots, onions, and cabbage
Scramble eggs
Add vegetables and cook until just cooked
Add in noodles and sauce
Mix well and serve
